## Risk Management

### Identified Risks & Mitigations
1. **Technical Complexity Risk:** Break down into smaller, manageable heartbeats
2. **Time Constraint Risk:** Prioritize critical path items, defer non-essential
3. **Dependency Risk:** Identify external dependencies early, have fallbacks
4. **Quality Risk:** Implement automated testing from day 1
5. **Scope Creep Risk:** Strictly adhere to MVP scope, document deviations

### Contingency Plans
1. **If behind schedule:** Extend by 1 day (April 3), maintain momentum
2. **If critical failure:** Rollback to last stable state, diagnose, fix
3. **If dependency blocked:** Implement mock/stub, continue development
4. **If quality issues:** Allocate time for bug fixing, don't sacrifice quality
5. **If motivation drops:** Celebrate small wins, maintain progress visibility
